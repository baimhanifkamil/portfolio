Emoji App

UI/UX, App, Sketch

The market are currently flooded with static emojis. Animated Emojis are just recently being pushed by big names like Facebook and WeChat.

However after a market research that my friend and I did,surprisingly there is no any animated emoji mobile apps in either Google Play Store nor Apple Store. The idea of the app is to showcase a few collections of animated emojis and make it super easy to be shared across different platform such as Whatsapp, text messsaging or even WeChat.
























This is one of the example of an animated emoji that I have created. The movements are minimal and the color are flat so that it won't use too much of user's bandwith.It is simple, yet gives a lot more depth compared to normal emojis.
